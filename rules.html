Rules

  • If cat card occured then the card will be removed from the deck.
  • If bomb card occured then you will loose the game.
  • If defuse card occured then the card will be removed and this card will automatically used in further if any bomb card occurs and continues the game.
  • If shuffle card occured then the deck will filled with another set of cards and the game restarts....

The player has to remove all the cards from deck and clears the deck to win
